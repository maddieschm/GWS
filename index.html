About us

Geeks Who Speak, PLLC is a company that provides a unique approach to mental health treatment. Our team currently has two licensed mental health counselors who specialize in providing therapy groups, workshops, and educational speaking engagements that cater to the geek community. We understand that the geek culture is a vibrant and diverse community that often faces unique challenges, and we are here to provide support and guidance to help our clients navigate these challenges.

Drama Therapy techniques

Drama therapy is a powerful tool for personal growth and healing. You can learn how to use drama and storytelling to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a safe and supportive environment. Through a series of fun and engaging exercises, you can gain insight into your own behavior and emotions, and develop new skills for managing stress, anxiety, and other challenges. Whether you are seeking personal growth, healing from trauma, or simply looking for a new way to express yourself, this workshop is the perfect opportunity to explore your creativity and find new ways of being. Join us and discover the transformative power of drama therapy!

Disability & Therapy

Disability is an important and often overlooked aspect of diversity in therapy. As therapists, it is crucial to understand and address the unique challenges and experiences of individuals with disabilities. This workshop will lead participants through a comprehensive exploration of disability in therapy, including the social and cultural factors that shape the experiences of individuals, the ethical considerations of working with this population, and practical strategies for creating inclusive and accessible therapy environments. Participants will gain a deeper understanding of the complex issues surrounding disability and develop the skills and knowledge necessary to provide culturally responsive and inclusive therapy services. Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your practice and better serve the needs of your clients.
